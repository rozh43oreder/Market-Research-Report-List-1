<p><p>Point-of-Care Coagulation Testing (POC CT) refers to diagnostic procedures performed at or near the site of patient care, facilitating rapid assessment of blood coagulation factors. This approach enhances patient management by providing immediate results, allowing for timely therapeutic interventions, particularly in settings like emergency rooms, surgical units, and outpatient clinics. POC CT devices measure various parameters such as PT, aPTT, and INR, crucial for monitoring anticoagulation therapy and managing bleeding disorders.</p><p>The Point-of-Care Coagulation Testing Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for rapid and efficient diagnostic tools, the rise in cardiovascular diseases, and a growing elderly population prone to clotting disorders. Technological advancements, such as the introduction of user-friendly, portable devices, further bolster market expansion. Additionally, strategic partnerships between key players and increasing investments in R&D enhance the availability and efficacy of POC CT solutions.</p><p>Furthermore, the point-of-care testing trend emphasizes decentralization of healthcare, increasing patient accessibility to diagnostic testing.
