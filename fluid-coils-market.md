<p><strong>The Fluid Coils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tubed Fluid Coils</li><li>Finned Fluid Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluid coils market consists of two primary types: tubed fluid coils and finned fluid coils. Tubed fluid coils feature simple tubing designs that facilitate fluid circulation for heating or cooling applications, making them ideal for compact systems. In contrast, finned fluid coils incorporate extended surfaces, or fins, that enhance heat transfer efficiency by increasing surface area, making them suitable for larger systems where rapid temperature changes are required. Both types are essential in HVAC and industrial applications, optimizing energy usage and performance.</p></p>
