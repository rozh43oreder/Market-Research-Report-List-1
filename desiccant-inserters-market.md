<p><p>The desiccant inserters market is primarily categorized into two types: canister and pouch systems. Canister inserters involve the use of rigid containers that hold desiccant materials, providing a longer-lasting moisture-absorbing solution, often suited for bulk packaging. Pouch inserters, on the other hand, utilize flexible, lightweight bags filled with desiccants, offering convenience and cost-effectiveness for smaller applications. Both types serve various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and electronics, to protect products from moisture damage during storage and transportation.</p></p>
