<p><strong>The Chemical Agricultural Colorants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dyes</li><li>Pigments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical agricultural colorants market is primarily divided into two types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are water-soluble compounds that penetrate plant tissues, providing vibrant colors and enhancing visual appeal, while pigments are insoluble particles that offer long-lasting color stability and UV protection. Dyes are often used for specific applications, such as marking and labeling, whereas pigments are preferred for their durability and resistance to environmental conditions. Both play crucial roles in improving product aesthetics and functionality in agriculture.</p></p>
